About Pentexa

Building the future of cybersecurity, one vulnerability at a time.

Our Story

Pentexa was founded by a team of security researchers and ethical hackers who were frustrated with the state of commercial security tools. We believed that world-class security shouldn't require a Fortune 500 budget.

Today, we serve hundreds of organizations worldwide — from startups to enterprises — helping them identify and fix vulnerabilities before attackers can exploit them. Our tools are built by pentesters, for pentesters.

Mission

Democratize cybersecurity by making enterprise-grade tools accessible to every organization, regardless of size.
